ORMUS Research Ideas

Inspiration struck this morning. I am taking a break from software development so I can use this page to talk about ORMUS/ORME research ideas. Once I know more about HTML I can make separate pages for the two topics, but for today I am writing this here.

During my ORMUS/ORME experiences I witnessed beautiful things. Beautiful things.

These materials, once properly understood and safely harnessed, hold the potential to completely change our way of life. These materials can change our existence, and they can do it on a deeply personal level.

They have the potential to do that. Whether or not they actually will is anyone’s guess. Like many super-good things, I wouldn’t be surprised if they were suppressed by existing power structures.

I think an elegant solution to that problem is to just change their minds… I truly think if “they” knew of the remarkable properties of these substances,and the wonderful experiences they facilitate, they would seek them out to have those benefits in their own lives.

But a keyword here is “safety.” After having my own ORMUS experiences, I think there are many questions that need to be answered before they can understood and safely harnessed.
